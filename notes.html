Creating new things is easy. Learning and growing to meet new goals is the tricky part.

I've gone through many portfolio iterations over the past few years. Each one builds on my knowledge of web development and design.

Every new version serves as a playground for my current development goals. This portfolio was an experiment in Illustrator vector design, which is my current pet project. It's gone through many changes: from moveable shelves, to flying bears, and finally--to airplanes.

This project is ongoing. As I learn new things and hone my skills, this playground of mine will change. The web is ever-evolving, and I continue to grow with it.

The skills that I perfect here will be applied to future projects, but my attention to detail and precision will never change. I aim to create the perfect online image through each page I design and develop. Little things make all the difference--whether it's for myself or my clients.
